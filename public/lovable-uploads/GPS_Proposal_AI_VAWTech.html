How It Works

Your Chatbot Updates Itself — Automatically

When you update content on your school website, the chatbot's knowledge base re-indexes automatically, so it always gives parents the latest fee or event information.

🔄
Auto Content Sync
Connect your website pages or Google Drive folder. Any update triggers a re-indexing pipeline — zero manual effort from your team.
🎯
Intent-Based Routing
The AI detects whether a visitor is asking about admissions, fees, academics, or general info — and routes to the right knowledge context.
⚡
Groq Fallback Guarantee
If the primary Gemini model experiences delays, the system automatically falls back to Groq's LLaMA-3 model with no interruption to the user experience. This ensures <2s response times even during peak school events like admissions season.
