05 · Social Media Automation

Your Website Updates Itself — Automatically

One of the biggest pain points for school websites is keeping content fresh. We solve this permanently with a smart social media automation layer — whenever GPS posts on Instagram, Facebook, or YouTube, it instantly appears on your website. No manual work, ever.

📸

Instagram Auto-Sync

Every photo and reel posted on GPS's Instagram account is automatically pulled into the website's gallery and news feed — organised by event, activity, or date. Your campus life page stays alive without lifting a finger.

📘

Facebook Post Integration

Facebook announcements, events, and updates sync directly to the website's news and events section. Parents see the same content in one place — no need to follow multiple platforms.

▶️

YouTube Video Embedding

GPS's YouTube videos — school tours, event coverage, student performances — are automatically featured on the website. Every new upload appears on the Video Gallery page without any manual action.

🔄

Unified Content Dashboard

A single admin dashboard lets the school team review, approve, or filter social content before it appears on the website. Full control, zero extra workload. Content can be tagged, categorised, and scheduled.

💡
How It Works
GPS posts on Instagram → Our integration fetches the post via API → Content is categorised automatically → It appears on the website within minutes. The same flow applies to Facebook and YouTube. GPS's social presence and website become one living, breathing content engine.
